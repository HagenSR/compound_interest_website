This website is for the computation, display and comparison of different compounding interest scenarios or simulations. Different than most compounding interest calculators is the inclusion of simulations and legs. Each simulation is calculated separately from another and each simulation can have multiple legs. A leg allows for different rates of saving, interest and duration.

To operate the website, simply enter valid data into each of the fields. Results will be automatically calculated when all fields are filled. Notice the chart at the bottom of the screen for further comparison of results.
